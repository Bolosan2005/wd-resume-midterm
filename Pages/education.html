Far Eastern University

Institute of Technology

FEU Institute of Technology (FEU Tech) is a private, non-sectarian institution located in Sampaloc, Manila, Philippines. Established in 1992 as the East Asia Computer Center, it focuses on Engineering and Computer Studies. Recognized as a Center of Development in IT Education by the Commission on Higher Education, FEU Tech is closely affiliated with Far Eastern University.

The institution is led by President Juan Miguel R. Montinola and Senior Executive Director Dr. Benson T. Tan. FEU Tech operates on a trimester system, with the academic year beginning in the second week of August and concluding in June, similar to its sister schools, FEU Diliman and FEU Alabang.
